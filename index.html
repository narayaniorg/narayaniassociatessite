Our Expertise

Practice Areas

Narayani Associates offers comprehensive legal services across multiple domains. Our attorneys are specialized in their respective fields to provide you with expert advice and representation.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

We provide end-to-end litigation services across civil, criminal, commercial, and constitutional matters. Whether it's a local trial or a high-stakes appeal, our team prepares every case with precision and confidence.

Corporate & Commercial Laws

We advise companies, startups, and investors on a wide range of legal issues impacting business operations. From incorporation to compliance — and everything in between.

Real Estate & RERA

Real estate transactions and disputes require a deep understanding of both law and land. We ensure your rights are protected whether you're buying, selling, leasing, or litigating under RERA.

Family & Succession Law

We handle delicate family law matters with discretion and firmness—ensuring legal clarity while respecting emotional sensitivities. Our expertise covers succession planning, inheritance, and family disputes.

Employment & Labour Law

We provide comprehensive legal support for employment matters, from contract drafting to dispute resolution, ensuring compliance with labor laws and protecting both employer and employee rights.

Banking & Finance

We assist in structuring, negotiating, and securing financial transactions, and represent clients in regulatory and recovery proceedings.
